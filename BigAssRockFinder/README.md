## Big Ass Rock Finder (B.A.R.F.)

The B.A.R.F. is a scintillation detector that utilizes a Hamamatsu R1513 photomultiplier tube, five-inch organic scintillator, and zinc-sulfide screen. It is designed specifically to quickly detect radioactive mineral specimens in large low-background environments.
